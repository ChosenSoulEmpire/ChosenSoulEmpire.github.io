About Us

Chosen Soul Empire offers practical tools, motivational encouragement, therapeutic art sessions, and faith-based teaching to propel your personal growth and ignite your journey to emotional healing. Do you feel like there are missing elements in your healing process? Despite attending retreats, reading numerous books, and seeking therapy, you may still feel like you lack practical tools or struggle to understand how to forgive from the heart and move forward. Perhaps unforgiveness is deeply rooted in your heart, leading to repeated cycles of emotional setbacks. We are dedicated to guiding you through a transformative journey of self-discovery and emotional healing, utilizing various artistic disciplines such as music, painting, drawing, mindfulness, sculpting, and writing.

Our aim is to heal one soul at a time through the transformative power of forgiveness, prayer, art, and creativity. We encourage you to unravel the principles of forgiveness through introspection, while tending to your mental health and rekindling your relationship with God. We firmly believe in one God in three persons the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. Our conviction lies in Jesus as the son of God, who sacrificed himself for our sins.

You are cordially invited to participate in our emotional healing paint party wellness workshops, conferences, or retreats. Our events are inclusive to all, regardless of your beliefs. We warmly welcome you to embark on this profound, loving, and transformative journey to address your pain and unforgiveness in a safe, creative, and spiritually uplifting environment.

Biblical Healing Formula

Matthew 13:15

For the hearts of these people are hardened, and their ears cannot hear, and they have closed their eyes so their eyes cannot see, and their ears cannot hear, and their hearts cannot understand, and they cannot turn to me and let me heal them.

Psalms 36:2-3

In their own eyes they flatter themselves too much to detect or hate their sin. The words of their mouths are wicked and deceitful; they fail to act wisely or do good.

The Bible is clear about why our healing might be delayed. We hinder our healing by refusing to see, hear, and understand the truth, and by not turning to God for healing. Instead, we might believe that we can handle things on our own, and we may replace the truth with lies. These lies may serve as a crutch for us to get through the day or to cling to anger and unforgiveness. We use these lies as a shield to protect ourselves.

To begin the healing process, we need to allow ourselves to be vulnerable with God. Our hearts must be open to hear, see, receive, and learn. Next, we need to listen clearly to what God is saying about our situation, which can be found in His Word and through our spiritual understanding. We also need to see the truth clearly in our situation. We should perceive the reality of the circumstance, our identity in Christ, and who God is, all from a godly perspective rather than a perspective clouded by brokenness.

In addition, we need to understand in our hearts what we hear from God and what we see in our situation, and then accept it. This means not relying on our own understanding or creating our own version of events. Understanding means hearing God, accepting it, and trusting Him. Lastly, we must turn to God and trust that His answer is sufficient. We must allow Him to lead us and heal us, with His word guiding our actions for change, growth, and healing.

Jay Lahens
